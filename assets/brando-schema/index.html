Brando® V1.0.0 – The Brand Definition Framework for Building AI-First Brands

Brando® — short for Brand Oracle — is a flexible brand definition schema built to optimise AI performance and govern behaviour. It extends Schema.org with a structured vocabulary that Large Language Models can interpret — enabling governance, policy alignment, and compliant automation across AI-driven environments. Brando® is the schema-based toolkit for building AI-native brands, safeguarding identity, and accelerating innovation.
